Travel Companion

Travel Companion addresses the inconvenience of constantly having to search for hotel and plane ticket reservations on email while travelling. It aims to compile all reservations in the same place to ensure seamless planning when on the go to your next destination.

Process

Worked with a partner on the concept and sketched out a lo-fi prototype. Then, conducted user tests with classmates. For the hi-fidelity prototypes, emphasis was placed on what information the user would want to have in handy while travelling.

Usability

To facilitate ease of use, we used many icons and succint text. We also aimed to make it a fluid experience so we established a cohesive visual language. One task is directly linked to the next on its description screen, allowing smooth transitions between events.
