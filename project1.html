E-Solidário

HUGE

ui, ux, interaction design

During the 2014 summer, I worked as a product design intern at Huge in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I worked closely with other interns to completely redesign an online platform aimed at connecting non-profit organizations to volunteers and donations.

Sitemap

Rethought the current website's sitemap to convert prospective users into donators. Segmented home page into two sections: one for users who already knew what they wanted to donate and the other for users who wanted to browse non-profit organizations.

Donation Flow Chart

Created separate flow charts for each type of donation: time (volunteers), goods and money. Designed lo-fi prototypes for each donation process.

Hi-Fidelity Prototypes

After designing the lo-fi prototypes and conducting user tests, we designed a cohesive visual language for the platform and created hi-fidelity prototypes to present to the stakeholders.
