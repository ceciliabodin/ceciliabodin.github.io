A Better World by Design

A Better World by Design is a student-led initiative at Brown University and Rhode Island School of Design that facilitates collaboration between designers, educators, innovators, and students in an annual three day conference. We empower attendees with tools to actively fulfill their visions of a sustainable and socially just future.
